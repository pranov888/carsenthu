THE STORY

The G-Power M5 F90 represents the pinnacle of BMW M5 tuning. Starting with the already formidable F90 M5, G-Power has elevated this super sedan to hypercar territory with a comprehensive power upgrade package.

The twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 receives upgraded turbochargers, a high-flow intercooler system, and custom engine management software, resulting in a staggering 800 horsepower output. The result is a four-door sedan that can embarrass supercars on any road or track.
